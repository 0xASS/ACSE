Job Description

As a Sales Engineer, you'll be the technical expert supporting our sales team. You'll demonstrate our product's capabilities, answer technical questions, and help design solutions that meet customer needs.

Responsibilities:

  • Conduct product demonstrations and technical presentations
  • Work with prospects to understand their technical requirements
  • Create proof‑of‑concept implementations
  • Respond to RFPs and technical questionnaires
  • Collaborate with product and engineering teams to relay customer feedback

Requirements:

  • 3+ years in a sales engineering or solutions architect role
  • Strong technical background (software development, cloud infrastructure)
  • Excellent presentation and communication skills
  • Ability to translate technical features into business value
  • Experience with enterprise SaaS sales cycles
