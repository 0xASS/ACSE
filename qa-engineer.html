Job Description

We're hiring a QA Engineer to ensure the quality and reliability of our software products. You'll design test plans, execute manual and automated tests, and work closely with developers to identify and resolve issues.

Responsibilities:

  • Create and execute detailed test cases and test plans
  • Perform manual testing across web and mobile platforms
  • Develop and maintain automated test scripts (Selenium, Cypress)
  • Report and track bugs using JIRA or similar tools
  • Participate in Agile ceremonies and provide quality insights

Requirements:

  • 2+ years of experience in software quality assurance
  • Knowledge of testing methodologies and QA best practices
  • Experience with test automation frameworks and tools
  • Familiarity with Agile/Scrum development processes
  • Strong attention to detail and analytical thinking
