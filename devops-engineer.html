Requirements:

  • Expertise in AWS (EC2, S3, RDS, Lambda, EKS)
  • Strong scripting skills (Bash, Python, Go)
  • Experience with containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
  • Knowledge of networking, security, and monitoring tools
